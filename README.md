# Facebook Ads Performance Dashboard 

This dashboard provides a comprehensive performance analysis of **Facebook Ad campaigns**, helping marketers and analysts track reach, engagement, spend efficiency, and conversions across time, age groups, and gender.
It is designed for **quick decision-making**, campaign optimization, and stakeholder reporting.

## Key Metrics (Top KPIs)

* **Total Impressions:** 79M
* **Total Spend:** $19.62K
* **Total Clicks:** 12K
* **Total Conversions:** 2K
* **CTR (Click Through Rate):** 1.49%

These KPIs give a high-level snapshot of campaign scale, cost, and effectiveness.

## Dashboard Sections & Insights

### 1. Performance Trend by Day

**Chart:** Line chart (Impressions, Clicks, Conversions)

* Tracks daily performance across August (17–30)
* Helps identify peak days, drops, and campaign consistency
* Useful for budget pacing and performance optimization

---

### 2. Total Spend by Age Group

**Chart:** Horizontal bar chart

* Highest spend: **Age 30–34 ($7.7K)**
* Followed by 35–39, 40–44, and 45–49
* Helps identify where the budget is concentrated

---

### 3. Conversion Rate by Age & Gender

**Chart:** Clustered bar chart

* Females consistently show **higher conversion rates** across all age groups
* Best performing segment: **Females aged 45–49 (2.79%)**
* Useful for audience targeting and bid optimization

---

### 4. Spend Distribution by Gender

**Chart:** Donut chart

* **Male:** $17.2K (88%)
* **Female:** $2.5K (12%)
* Indicates spend skewed heavily toward male audience

---

### 5. Conversion Rate by Gender

**Chart:** Donut chart

* **Female:** 65% of total conversions
* **Male:** 35% of total conversions
* Suggests females convert better despite lower spend

---

### 6. Total Impressions by Gender

**Chart:** Donut chart

* **Male:** 72.0M (92%)
* **Female:** 6.5M (8%)
* Confirms higher reach among male audience
